About This Analysis

📥

Step 1: Download

Downloaded auto-generated subtitles from all 131 videos on the Predictive History YouTube channel using yt-dlp.

🧹

Step 2: Clean

Cleaned VTT subtitle files — removed duplicates, overlapping text, and timestamps — producing 1.4M words of readable transcript across 131 files.

🔍

Step 3: Extract

Read 9 of the most prediction-dense transcripts and extracted forward-looking claims, analytical frameworks, and specific forecasts.

🔗

Step 4: Deduplicate

Merged overlapping predictions that appeared across multiple videos into 33 unique, distinct predictions.

⚖️

Step 5: Fact-Check

Evaluated each prediction against publicly known events as of March 2026, scoring them as correct, partially correct, wrong, pending, or unfalsifiable.

🌐

Step 6: Publish

Built this website to make the analysis publicly browsable, searchable, and interactive.
